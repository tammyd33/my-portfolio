Chicken Trolley Animation

This project is character animation showing how a chicken crosses the road.
The animation idea came from an ad that I created for a Trolley system used in
chicken houses to make transporting different items throughout the length of a
commercial chicken house easier.
I used a motion tween to move the cloud and trolley while using a classic tween
to make the chicken get onto and off of the trolley. Using keyframes, I inserted
outlined text that I copied from Illustrator.
