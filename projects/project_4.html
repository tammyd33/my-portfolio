Magazine Cover

For this project I chose to create a magazine cover. The different fonts and the way they are layered with the photos and images on magazines has always sparked my interest, so this project choice was easy.
Since the theme for my magazine is art & graphic design, the image I chose for the cover featured an artist next to a framed piece of art. The art in the frame was a bit too subtle for my vision, so I used the skew transform tool and added some filters to replace the image. I felt like the empty space next to the artist was taking away from the subject of my main article so I enlarged the photo to fill up the page better, but that left a gap on the right side of the document. Using generative fill, I was able to extend the photo to fill the gap nicely. I wanted a bit of dimension so I masked out a small part of the a and r in the title to make it appear to be behind the artist. The barcode was created using a free online barcode generator and downloaded as a GIF. I wanted the barcode to match the text color so I masked out the white background and added a color layer style.
